How to Register

 

1. Open 'Click to Register' link to create user account.
2. Enter all the details and submit the form. 
3. Once account is created, sign in and upload the abstract in docx or pdf format under ‘Under Abstract Status’ section. The abstract template is available on the “Abstract and Awards” page.
4. After receiving abstract acceptance notification on email, pay the requisite registration fees online. Upload the online transactions details with transaction ID. (Bank details will be updated soon).
5. Receipt of Registration fee payment will be provided at the conference venue.
